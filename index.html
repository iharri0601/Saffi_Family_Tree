Saffi's family

I never knew Saffi's true origin just like Jimmy Two-shoe and Helouse, so I made up a family tree with different characters.

  • Boros was the father of Saffi after he met her mother, Xena the warrior princess.
  • Boros met Xena on a distant planet far from Earth.
  • They both battle with each other and the result was a draw. They later fell in love and ended the war.
  • Saffi was born on the Xena's homeworld later on. Xena later finds out that Boros would use her daughter as a weapon of Mass destruction. She send Saffi away to miseryville before died by Boros' hand. Saff grew up as a scavanger eating out of trashcans until she met Jimmy Two-Shoes and Beezy.
